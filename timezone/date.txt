%d %B %Y %R

  Category 	Code	Description	Example
Year	%Y	Year with century	2026
%y	Year without century (zero-padded)	26
Month	%B	Full month name	April
%b	Abbreviated month name	Apr
%m	Month as a zero-padded decimal	04
Day	%d	Day of the month (zero-padded)	06
%j	Day of the year (001–366)	096
Weekday	%A	Full weekday name	Monday
%a	Abbreviated weekday name	Mon
%w	Weekday as decimal (0=Sunday, 6=Saturday)	1
Time	%H	Hour (24-hour clock, zero-padded)	14
%I	Hour (12-hour clock, zero-padded)	02
%M	Minute (zero-padded)	05
%S	Second (zero-padded)	09
%p	Locale's equivalent of AM or PM	PM
Other	%Z	Time zone name	UTC
%z	UTC offset (+HHMM or -HHMM)	+0000
%%	A literal '%' character	%
